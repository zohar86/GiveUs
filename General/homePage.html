Welcome to GIVE-US

About as:

This website was created to save surplus food and donate to needy people.
Every year are throwing away 2.6 million tons of food and 50% of them are eatable.
We would like to save the surplus by connecting big organizations like factories, Event halls, Catering companies and etc. to Associations that provide hot meals to needy people.
We also want to connect small organizations like restaurants, coffee houses, bakeries, and supermarkets to the private needy through our website.
